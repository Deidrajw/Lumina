How to use Lumina — tap to expand
🔍 Symptoms
Short-term experiences you log when they happen. Examples: bloating, headache, skin dryness, breakout, fatigue, eye twitch.
🛡️ Concerns
Bigger, ongoing issues you want to monitor over time. Examples: persistent bloating, recurring chin breakouts, hair shedding, sleep issues.
📦 Vault / Products
Your product library — skincare, supplements, prescriptions, haircare, bodycare. Active products show in your routines. Archive finished ones to keep history.
📋 Protocols
Scheduled wellness actions — your AM/PM skincare, supplements by timing, hair wash days, prescriptions. Check off each step daily.
🔔 Reminders
Auto-generated from your data: expiring products, low stock, overdue follow-ups, Rx refill needed. Tap Done when resolved, Dismiss to hide.
📊 Insights / Timeline
Insights shows patterns from your logged data. Timeline is a chronological history of everything you've done in the app.
